Prague

Getting to and from the airport

To get a taxi from outside the airport termainal it should cost around 600kč. However it is very easy to do this by public transport. In the arrivals hall head to the Prague Public Transport Authority desk to get a public transport ticket (costs 32kč). Get on the 119 bus (that runs every 10 minutes), the journey should take about 20 minutes to the Nádraží Veleslavín merto stop (line A). Continue by metro for another 10 to 15 minutes (all on the same ticket that you got at the airport). The maps in the metro train have drawings on them a certain stops to show you what attractions are there, this comes in handy when you're trying to work out what stop you need to get off at.
